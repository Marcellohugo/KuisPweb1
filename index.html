Bekasi City

Bekasi City (Sundanese script: ᮘᮨᮊᮞᮤ) is a city in West Java Province, Indonesia. The name Bekasi comes from the word Bagasasi which means the same as Candrabaga which is written in the Tugu Inscription from the Tarumanegara Kingdom era, namely the name of the river that passes through this city.
